We have just released a new version in Netty 3 branch - 3.10.1.Final.

### 3.10.1.Final

* [#3326](https://github.com/netty/netty/issues/3326) and [#3327](https://github.com/netty/netty/pull/3327) - HTTP multipart request with filename containing ";" causes exception
* [#3504](https://github.com/netty/netty/issues/3504) - Privacy violation in io.netty.handler.codec.socks.SocksAuthRequest
* [#3508](https://github.com/netty/netty/pull/3508) - Safely encode Strings to ASCII in HttpObjectEncoder

You can also browse our issue tracker  ([3.10.1.Final](https://github.com/netty/netty/issues?q=milestone%3A3.10.1.Final)) for the gory detail.

As always, please let us know if you find something to fix, improve, discuss, or even criticize.  We love your feedback!
